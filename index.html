FAB DAY 2019

Come enjoy this robo-week celebration aimed at robotics enthusiasts and others. DROID 2019, scheduled on the 30th of April and the 1st of May 2019 is the first edition of this event.

Register now

About

Every moment is an organizing opportunity, every person a potential activist, every minute a chance to change the world. 26th of April is going to one such moment that you definitely do not want to miss . Arent we curious to know deep about our future technologies like AR,VR , Machine learning etc? Are there some of you who have already developed in these fields and wants to exhibit it to the rest of the world ? Well , here is your golden chance to make this all happen. Fab day brings you some very fascinating workshops , talks and much more .

  • VR Show

    Have you ever heard about VR, a.k.a VIRTUAL REALITY?? Have you seen people wear a headset covering their eyes and being fascinated by what they see? Thats exactly what VR is.

    VR is an immersion experience that involves putting a user into a digital space with 3-dimensions. Still confused?
    Fab day brings you this wonderful platform for you to experience the wonders of Virtual reality,VR SHOW.

    It's going to be one hell of an experience . Do not miss it.
